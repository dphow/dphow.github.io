Donation Recommendations

Beyond David's passions in music and construction, David was also attentive to the needs of imigrant populations. He was known to support Exodus Refugee which offers transitional services and housing for incoming refugees to settle into a life of dignity in Indiana.

Click here to donate to the Exodus Refugee organization.
