Support Music Education in South Bend City Schools

Linda Howard had an extensive collection of string instruments that we plan to donate to SBCSC. We ask that you consider doing the same. We are working to establish a fund to support music education for inner city youth via the South Bend Education Foundation. So far, you may utilize and share Linda's Go Fund Me Campaign. Please contact Daniel Howard if you would like to help make this campaign a success. Otherwise, please send your donation to the above Go Fund Me Campaign or ideally directly to the below address:

SBCSC Fine Arts, Administration Building
215 S. Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Blvd.
South Bend, IN 46601
